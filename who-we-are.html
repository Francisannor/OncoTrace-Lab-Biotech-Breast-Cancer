Who We Are

The OncoTrace Lab & Biotech is a student-driven initiative that is dedicated to breast cancer research.

We focus on early detection, diagnostics, and community education to make a meaningful impact.

Join us in our mission to transform breast cancer outcomes through innovation and awareness.

Mission

Our mission is to empower communities through early breast cancer detection, promote accessible screening technologies, and translate biotech innovation into real-world clinical impact.

Vision

We envision a future where no woman dies from late detection of breast cancer. By pioneering affordable diagnostics and fostering awareness, we aim to revolutionize breast cancer outcomes globally.

Our goal is to develop a wearable breast cancer screening technology for early breast cancer biomarker detection.

Research & Impact

We are currently embarking on a research with the Lumiere Research Scholars Program. Our research focuses on biomarker discovery, molecular diagnostics, imaging tools, and early detection strategies. By collaborating with hospitals, research institutions, and community organizations, we ensure our work translates into meaningful and measurable impact.
