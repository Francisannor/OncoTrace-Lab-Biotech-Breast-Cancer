How to Perform a Breast Self-Exam

Performing a self-exam monthly can help detect changes early. Follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and look for changes in shape, size, or skin texture.
  2. Raise your arms and look for changes.
  3. Lie down and use your opposite hand to check your breast for lumps.
  4. Repeat the exam monthly.
